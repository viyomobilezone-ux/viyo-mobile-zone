About Rajan Mobiles

Rajan Mobiles is built as a modern neighborhood tech hub where real‑time guidance, honest pricing and long‑term support come together.

From simple sales counter to smart experience center

Rajan Mobiles started with a clear idea – customers should not feel confused while buying or repairing a smartphone. Every visitor is welcomed with a structured explanation of models, pricing and practical usage, so the final decision is comfortable and transparent.

Over time, the store has evolved into a complete experience center with live demo units, accessory walls, EMI options and a full‑fledged service desk. The focus is to create long‑term relationships, not just one‑time sales.

Strong backend service team

Behind the showroom, the dedicated service team handles display, battery, chip‑level and software level work using industry‑grade tools. Each job is recorded with a ticket ID and status updates, keeping customers informed at every step.

The technicians follow ESD‑safe handling practices and use quality‑checked spares. The team regularly updates skills on new chipsets, 5G devices and security features to handle the latest smartphone platforms confidently.

What you can expect when you visit

Every process at Rajan Mobiles is designed to be simple, traceable and customer‑friendly, whether you are buying your first smartphone or upgrading after many years.

Clear demo of features

Team members explain cameras, battery backup, storage, performance and warranty in plain language, matching each model to your daily usage pattern.

Transparent repair workflow

Every device is checked in front of you, issue is noted in the job sheet, and you receive a time estimate before work begins.

After‑sales support

Even after purchase or repair, the store assists with setup, backup, app installation and basic training for new users.
