Mobile service and repair solutions

The Viyo Mobile Zone service lab handles everything from cracked screens to deep chip‑level faults, with a focus on data care and quality parts.

Replacement of broken, flickering or unresponsive displays using compatible panels and proper frame fitting for a factory‑like finish.

Battery & charging issues

Diagnosis of battery health, charging connector replacement and power‑related faults for better backup and safe charging.

Chip‑level board repairs

Flashing official firmware, resolving boot loops, removing unwanted malware and optimizing performance without disturbing your personal data whenever possible.
